Privacy Policy
Effective Date: August 16, 2025 Gosple go is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our games and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use the Game:

1.1 Personal Information
- Information you provide during account creation or authentication, such as your email address, username, or other identifiers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (e.g., device type, operating system, browser type).
- Gameplay data (e.g., progress, achievements, and in-game actions).

1.3 Third-Party Services
- If you use third-party authentication (e.g., Unity Player Accounts), we may collect information provided by those services.


2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
- To provide and improve the Game and related services.

- To analyze gameplay data for improving user experience.
- To comply with legal obligations.


3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following cases:
- With Service Providers:
We may share data with third-party services like Unity Authentication to enable features such as account management and analytics.
- For Legal Compliance:
We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.


4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Game and related services or as required by law.


5. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:
- Access, update, or delete your personal information.
- Opt-out of data collection or processing.
- Contact us to exercise your rights.


6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
However, no system is completely secure.


7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.
If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.


8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.


9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: admin@gospelgo.org
